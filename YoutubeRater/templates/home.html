YOUTUBE RATER

Monitoring YouTube Videos

We have created a system for parents to help protect their kids through monitoring and blocking what videos they watch on YouTube. Parents create an account with our website and link it to their children's Google accounts. They configure what videos should be permitted. Then, after installing a Chrome Extension on their children's devices, some videos will automatically be blocked and reported to you in an email.
